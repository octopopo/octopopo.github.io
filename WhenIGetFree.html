When I Get Free - Act II

Game

"When I Get Free" is a game that says a story about a characters, who got prisoned for the crime he commited. To brake the prison, the team should finish their job perfectly. The Act II of the game is a comic like game, players should put the pieces in the right order to create the path out of the prison.

My Role

As the lead engineer of this project, I need to keep checking with the producer to see if the features meet requirements. Moreover, I am responsible of designing the whole mechanic of the puzzle

Below are my works:
1. Puzzle System: Programmed the mechanic of using mouse as control to drag and drop puzzle pieces. The system also included the function of checking the answer and giving corresponding hint to assit player to solve the puzzles.

2. Level Editor: Implemented a level editor script, so that desinger can simply do the level design with setting number of pieces and the script will automatically place them in the right position.
