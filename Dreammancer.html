Dreammancer

Game

"Dreammancer" is a 2D platformer game using color as the core mechanic. By switching the color of the shield, player can destroy monster or avoid traps if they match the color. With three levels, including a boss level, Dreammancer is a excited experience.

My Role

Below are my works:

1. Designed the color mechanic: Proposed the idea of using simple color matching idea as our core. I want our mechanic to be intuitive to player so that they can easily enjoy without any guidance.

2. Set up the whole boss level: Designed and implemented the boss level. Scripted the behavior of our final boss. Conducted numerous playtest to make sure the level can be challenging while keep the fun.
