I Hear You

Game

"I Hear You" is a "sound" puzzle game. Basically, you put pieces in order not by what you see, but what you hear. It's a story about a little girl who just lost her love one. Deeply sad she is, she founds out that she can receive message from her love one by listening to the star. As a player, you will move the little girl, to find out the perfect distance that you can hear the message clearly from different stars. Each star contains a syllable, by pressing the star to give them the correct order, you can help the little girl to find out the secret.

Since we are a diversity group, our game included sounds from seven different languages.
