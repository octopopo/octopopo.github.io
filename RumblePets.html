Eumble Pets

Game

"Rumble Pets" is an AR game on the new headset "Magic Leap One". The idea of this game is making our childhood dream of raising our own pet and battle with friend. What's even better, to have all these things happen in front of us. With the power of Magic Leap One, we can mix the reality with virtual character. So player can have an experience of having a real pet and play with it.

My Role

I am an engineer in Teddie And I. As part of the engineering team, I work closely with game designers. Aside from implementing their idea into reality, I am aware of efficiency as well as clarity of my work. Additionally, to help designer understand my work faster, I wrote detailed documents including class description, and how to set up. I enjoy brainstorming with my colleague and making magic with my team.

Below are my works:
1. Checkpoint System: A system that can help player keeps their progress when they died. So that everytime Erin dies, he don't have to start again from the very beginning. Instead of reset the game progress by reloading the whole game. I developed a resetable interface, so that each check point would know what objects in the scene would need to be reset, and it will reset them directly.

2. Pushable Box and Pressure Plate: One of the puzzle mechanic the game. At some point of the game, Erin and Teddie would need to either stand on the pressure plate by themselves or push a box onto it to progress through the game.
