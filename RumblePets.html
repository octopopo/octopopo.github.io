Rumble Pets

Game

"Rumble Pets" is an AR game on the new headset "Magic Leap One". The idea of this game is making our childhood dream of raising our own pet and battle with friend. What's even better, to have all these things happen in front of us. With the power of Magic Leap One, we can mix the reality with virtual character. So player can have an experience of having a real pet and play with it.

My Role

Since we are a small team. So I am in charge of both programming and some designing work.

Below are my works:

1. Developing Stats System: Set up the pipeline for "Rumble Pets" to read user information as well as the pets data from local .json files. Moreover, it will update the .json file every time users exit the game. Wrote aiding editor script, so the team can make quick changes to testing files quickly.

2. Assisting with design of battle system: Helped in the design of our battle system propotype. Proposed the idea of using two basics attributes, stamina and strengh to determine the battle result.
