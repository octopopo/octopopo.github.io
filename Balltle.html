Balltle

Game

"Balltle" is a six person battle royale game, which spawns player in a maze. With random generated power-ups, player would need to choose their strategy. Whether they can go to the dangerous zone with multiple power-ups, or they can choose the safe path with less power-ups. No matter which way they play, the last man standing wins.

My Role

Below are my works:
1. Power-up System: Designed and coded the power-up system, player can pick up the item distributed in the battle field to upgrade their weapons. Futhermore, with randomly generated mechaic, every game will be a different experience.

2. Kill Count and Kill Notification: Inspired by Overwatch, I introduced the kill count and kill notification system to our game. Whenever a player dies, the system will broadcast the killing message to each player through network.
